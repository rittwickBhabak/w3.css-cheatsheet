Padding

w3-padding-32 adds 32px top and bottom padding to element
w3-padding-48 adds 48px top and bottom padding to element
w3-padding-64 adds 64px top and bottom padding to element
'w3-padding-small' add 4px top-bottom and 8px left-right padding to element

'w3-padding-large' add 12px top-bottom and 24px left-right padding to element
